# Subscription plans, pricing, and changing your plan

CloudCart has three subscription plans. You can change your plan at any time from
**Billing -> Subscription**, and a plan change never deletes your data — your
order history, products, and settings all carry over.

## The three plans

- **Starter** — $29 per month. Up to 100 orders per month, one staff account, and
  the standard checkout. Best for a new store finding its footing.
- **Growth** — $79 per month. Unlimited orders, five staff accounts, abandoned-cart
  recovery, and the full analytics dashboard. The plan most stores settle on.
- **Scale** — $199 per month. Everything in Growth, plus priority support, custom
  roles, the webhooks API, and multi-currency checkout.

Annual billing takes two months off any plan (pay for ten, get twelve).

## Changing your plan

Open **Billing -> Subscription** and click **Change plan**. Pick the new plan and
confirm. The change takes effect immediately.

- **Upgrading** (for example Starter to Growth) is prorated: you are charged the
  difference for the rest of the current cycle right away, and the new features
  unlock at once.
- **Downgrading** (for example Growth to Starter) takes effect at the START of
  your next billing cycle, so you keep the features you already paid for until
  then. Nothing is lost — downgrading does not touch your order history or
  exported data.

## Will I lose my order history if I downgrade?

No. Your order history is part of your store, not your plan. Downgrading reduces
your monthly order ALLOWANCE and removes plan-specific features (for instance,
Scale's webhooks API), but every past order stays in **Orders** and remains
exportable under **Settings -> Data & Privacy -> Export data**. If you want a
backup before changing plans, export your orders first; the export is unaffected
by the plan change.

## Cancelling

Cancelling stops the subscription at the end of the current cycle. Your store
goes read-only — you keep access to view and export data, but cannot take new
orders until you resubscribe. Data is retained for 90 days after cancellation.
